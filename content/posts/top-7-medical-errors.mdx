From wrong-site surgery to medication overdoses, common errors often produce the clearest evidence of negligence. Documenting how the error impacted long-term care and cost is central to maximizing recovery.

Top errors to watch: careless medication dosing, delayed diagnosis, surgical mistakes, and failure to communicate risks.

Short tip: Request all records and a detailed timeline from the treating facility — it's the starting point for any malpractice case.
